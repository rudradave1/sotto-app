# Privacy Policy - Sotto

**Last updated:** July 1, 2026

Sotto is a privacy-first voice memory app. This Privacy Policy explains what information is processed when you use the app and how your data is handled.

## Information Stored on Your Device

Sotto stores your memories locally on your device.

This includes:

* Memory transcripts
* AI-generated titles, tags, categories, moods, and reflections
* App preferences and settings

All stored memories are encrypted locally using SQLCipher.

Sotto does **not** permanently store your audio recordings. Audio files are temporary and are automatically deleted after processing.

---

## Accurate Mode (Optional)

If you choose **Accurate** mode, your voice recording is securely sent to Groq for speech transcription and AI-powered memory organization.

Groq provides **Zero Data Retention (ZDR)** for supported APIs. Audio and text are processed only to generate your transcription and structured memory and are not retained or used for model training.

For more information, please see Groq's Privacy Policy:
https://groq.com/privacy-policy

---

## Offline Mode

If you choose **Offline** mode, transcription is performed entirely on your device using Vosk.

No audio, transcript, or memory content is transmitted over the internet.

---

## Information We Do Not Collect

Sotto does not:

* Create user accounts
* Require login
* Collect personal information
* Track your activity
* Use analytics or advertising SDKs
* Sell or share your personal data
* Access your stored memories

---

## Permissions

Sotto requests the following permission:

**Microphone**

* Required to record voice memories.

No microphone data is recorded unless you explicitly start recording.

---

## Data Deletion

Your memories remain on your device.

You can permanently delete memories at any time within the app or by uninstalling Sotto, which removes all locally stored app data.

---

## Children's Privacy

Sotto is intended for adults and is not directed toward children.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be available with the app.
