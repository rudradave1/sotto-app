Privacy Policy - Sotto

Last updated: June 2026

Sotto is a private voice journaling app. This policy explains 
what data we collect and how we use it.

DATA STORED ON YOUR DEVICE
Journal entries, transcripts, and audio recordings are stored 
locally on your device in an encrypted database. This data 
never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose 
Accurate transcription mode.

ACCURATE MODE (OPTIONAL)
If you select Accurate mode, audio is sent to Groq's API for 
transcription. Groq operates under a Zero Data Retention policy — 
audio is processed and immediately discarded. It is never stored 
or used for model training. See [groq.com/privacy](https://groq.com/privacy-policy) for details.

OFFLINE MODE
In Offline mode, all transcription happens on your device. 
No audio or text is ever transmitted to any server.

DATA WE DO NOT COLLECT
• We do not collect personal information
• We do not use advertising or tracking SDKs
• We do not sell or share your data
• We do not have access to your journal entries
